Privacy Policy

Telugu Bible Application

Overview

Telugu Bible is designed to provide a simple and completely offline Bible reading experience. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Telugu Bible does not collect, store, or share any personal user data.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal Information
  • Location Data
  • Contacts
  • Payment Information
  • Passwords or Login Details
  • Usage Tracking Data

Account & Login

Telugu Bible does not require user registration, sign-in, or account creation.

Sharing Bible Verses

Users may optionally share Bible verses using their device’s built-in sharing features such as WhatsApp, Gmail, or other apps.

Telugu Bible does not access, monitor, track, or store any shared content.

Offline Usage

Telugu Bible works primarily offline, allowing users to read scripture without requiring continuous internet access.
