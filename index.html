Privacy Policy

Telugu Bible Application

Overview

Telugu Bible is designed to provide a simple, easy-to-use, and mostly offline Bible reading experience. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your information.

Telugu Bible does not require account creation and does not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

Information We Collect

Most features of Telugu Bible work completely offline. However, the app may use certain third-party services that collect limited information to improve functionality and support the application.

  • Advertising identifiers used by Google AdMob to serve advertisements.
  • Device information required for app performance and ad delivery.
  • Anonymous analytics and diagnostic information provided by third-party services, if enabled.

Google AdMob Advertising

Telugu Bible uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and process certain information, including advertising identifiers, device information, and app interaction data to provide and measure advertisements.

For more information about how Google collects and processes data, please review Google's Privacy Policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Bookmarks and Notes

Bookmarks, notes, reading preferences, and saved Bible content are stored locally on your device.

Telugu Bible does not upload, access, or share your bookmarks, notes, or reading history.

Verse Sharing

Users may optionally share Bible verses using their device’s built-in sharing features such as WhatsApp, Gmail, Messages, Facebook, or other applications.

Telugu Bible does not access, monitor, track, or store any content that users choose to share.

Verse of the Day & Notifications

Telugu Bible may display daily Bible verses and send local reminder notifications to encourage regular Bible reading.

These notifications are generated locally by the application and do not require collection of personal information.

Text-to-Speech

The Read Aloud feature uses your device's built-in Text-to-Speech engine to read Bible chapters aloud.

Bible text processed through Text-to-Speech is not collected, stored, or transmitted by Telugu Bible.

Account & Login

Telugu Bible does not require registration, sign-in, or account creation.

Offline Usage

Most Bible reading features are available offline and do not require an active internet connection.

Children's Privacy

Telugu Bible is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page along with an updated revision date.
