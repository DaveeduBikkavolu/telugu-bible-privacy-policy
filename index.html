Privacy Policy

Telugu Bible Application

Overview

Telugu Bible is designed to provide a simple, easy-to-use, and mostly offline Bible reading experience in both Telugu and English (World English Bible - WEB).

Telugu Bible does not require user registration and does not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

Information We Collect

Most features work completely offline. However, the application uses trusted third-party services to support advertisements and improve app performance.

  • Advertising identifiers.
  • Device information.
  • Crash reports and anonymous diagnostics (when available through third-party services).
  • App performance information.

Google AdMob Advertising

Telugu Bible displays banner and interstitial advertisements using Google AdMob. Advertising revenue helps support the continued development and maintenance of the application.

Google AdMob may collect information such as advertising identifiers, IP address, device information, and app interaction data for personalized or non-personalized advertisements and ad performance measurement.

Learn more by visiting Google's Privacy Policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Bookmarks & Notes

Your bookmarks, notes, reading preferences, and saved Bible content are stored only on your device.

Telugu Bible does not upload, synchronize, or share this information with any server.

Verse Sharing

You may share Bible verses using your device's built-in sharing features such as WhatsApp, Gmail, Messages, Facebook, or other compatible applications.

Telugu Bible does not monitor, access, or store the content that you choose to share.

Verse of the Day & Notifications

Telugu Bible can send a daily "Verse of the Day" notification to encourage regular Bible reading.

These notifications are generated locally on your device and do not require the collection or transmission of personal information.

Notification permission is used only to deliver these reminders. You may disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

Text-to-Speech

The Read Aloud feature uses your device's built-in Text-to-Speech engine to read Bible chapters aloud.

Bible text processed through Text-to-Speech is never collected, stored, or transmitted by Telugu Bible.

Offline Usage

Most Bible reading features are available completely offline after installation.

Internet Usage

Internet access is required only for displaying advertisements, checking for app updates, and opening external links such as Google Play and this Privacy Policy.

Account & Login

Telugu Bible does not require account creation, login, or registration.

Children's Privacy

Telugu Bible is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be published on this page along with the updated revision date.
