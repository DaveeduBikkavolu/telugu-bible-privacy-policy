Privacy Policy

Telugu Bible is an offline Bible application and does not collect, store, or share personal information.

The app does not require user registration or login.

Users may optionally share Bible verses using the device's built-in sharing features. Shared content is handled by the apps selected by the user (such as WhatsApp, Gmail, etc.). Telugu Bible does not store or track shared content.

We do not collect:

  • Personal data
  • Location information
  • Contacts
  • Payment information
